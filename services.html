Our Technical Capabilities

Precision services for the most demanding applications.

Bespoke Gear Forging

We specialize in the creation of custom gears tailored to your exact specifications. Our process begins with a deep dive into your application's requirements, ensuring the final product delivers optimal performance, longevity, and reliability. We transform high-grade materials into precision-engineered components.

  • Spur & Helical Gears: For parallel shaft power transmission.
  • Bevel & Miter Gears: For changing rotational direction.
  • Worm Gears & Shafts: For high-ratio speed reduction.
  • Internal Gears & Splines: For compact, high-torque applications.

Reverse Engineering

Legacy systems often rely on parts that are no longer in production. Our reverse engineering service is the solution. We meticulously deconstruct and analyze existing components to recreate them with modern materials and manufacturing techniques, often improving upon the original design for enhanced performance and durability.

  • 3D Laser Scanning: Capturing precise geometry of complex parts.
  • CAD Model Recreation: Building production-ready digital blueprints.
  • Material Analysis: Identifying original alloys for accurate replication.
  • Prototyping & Validation: Ensuring perfect fit and function before full production.

Design & Prototyping

From a simple sketch to a fully-realized concept, our engineering team collaborates with you to design and prototype innovative gearing solutions. We utilize advanced simulation software to test for stress, load, and wear, ensuring your design is viable and optimized before any metal is cut.

  • FEA & Stress Analysis: Simulating real-world conditions digitally.
  • Rapid Prototyping: Creating functional models for physical testing.
  • Gearbox Design: Engineering complete transmission systems.
  • Material & Treatment Consultation: Selecting the best options for your needs.

Quality & Materials

The foundation of a superior gear is superior material and uncompromising quality control.

Premium Alloys

We source a wide range of certified materials, including carbon steel, stainless steel, bronze, and exotic alloys for specialized applications.

Metrological Inspection

Our climate-controlled metrology lab uses CMMs and advanced optical scanners to verify every dimension to sub-micron tolerances.

Full Traceability

We provide comprehensive documentation and certification for materials, heat treatments, and inspection results for every project.
