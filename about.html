Our Journey Through Time

Four decades of innovation, precision, and unwavering commitment.

1982

Standard Gear Manufacturing is founded in a small Portland workshop by Johnathan Crane, a master machinist with a vision for unparalleled quality.

1995

Investment in our first 5-axis CNC machine, revolutionizing our capabilities and allowing for the creation of highly complex geometries.

2008

Expansion into our current facility on SE Ochoco St, tripling our floor space and establishing an in-house metallurgical lab.

2020

Awarded "Manufacturer of the Year" by the Pacific Northwest Aerospace Alliance for our contributions to the sector.

Today

Continuing our legacy of excellence, we embrace new technologies like additive manufacturing while staying true to our core principles of precision and partnership.

The Soul of the Machine

Our philosophy is simple: perfection is the only acceptable standard. We believe that behind every great machine is a set of perfectly crafted gears, and that responsibility inspires us. It's a belief forged in the heat of the furnace and polished on the factory floor.

We are not just suppliers; we are partners in creation. Our workshop is a sanctuary of precision, where technology and craftsmanship coexist to turn ambitious designs into tangible, reliable components.
